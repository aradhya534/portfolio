DermaScan is a web-based application designed to help users monitor and understand their skin health. It allows users to upload or scan images of their skin, providing AI-assisted analysis to detect potential conditions and give informative feedback.

The platform includes secure user authentication, enabling personalized experiences and maintaining records of scans, medical history, and chat interactions. Users can also access educational articles and resources about various skin conditions, making it a comprehensive tool for skin health awareness.

Built with Django and REST APIs, DermaScan combines a user-friendly interface with robust backend functionality. Its interactive features, including an AI-powered chatbot, ensure users receive guidance and support in managing their skin health effectively.
